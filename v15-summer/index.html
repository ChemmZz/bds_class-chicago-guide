Summer

Lakefront Brightness · Sky Blue · July Sun · Fraunces Variable

Concept

July on the Lakefront Trail. Northerly Island is packed. The Taste of Chicago is loud. Oak Street Beach has been open since Memorial Day. The entire city has migrated outside. This is the version of Chicago that people who have never been here don't know exists.

Fraunces is a variable optical-size serif that looks almost like a slab at heavy optical sizes and almost like a transitional roman at small sizes. At opsz=144 weight=900, the display text has the outdoor sign energy of a festival poster.

Design Decisions

Maximum softness border-radius: 6/12/20/999px throughout. Every element is rounded — the opposite of Brutalist or Gothic. Summer is soft-edged.
Active tag glow box-shadow: 0 4px 12px rgba(42,128,200,0.35) on active filter tags. The glow is the only light effect in this iteration — one deliberate touch of sunshine.
Saturated map saturate(1.15) brightness(1.06) hue-rotate(2deg) — colors pop like a summer photograph with the contrast cranked up a touch.
Sky blue route-info bar Solid var(--accent) background with white text — the blue bar floats above the map like a lifeguard flag.
