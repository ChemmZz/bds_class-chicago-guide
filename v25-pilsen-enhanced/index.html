Concept

V25 takes V18 Pilsen — the preferred base palette — and refines three details without touching the underlying system. The header title gains explicit weight. The four stars honor the Chicago city flag. And thrift stores join the filter set as a seventh category, sitting alongside architecture, music, nightlife, jazz, doors, and café.

No new palette, no new fonts, no decorative header imagery. Enhancement means precision: each change is targeted and intentional.

Design Decisions

Bold CHICAGO Abril Fatface is already a display weight, but adding font-weight: 900 pushes the browser to render it at maximum available boldness. The title earns its position as the typographic anchor of the header.
Red stars — Chicago flag The four stars reference the Chicago municipal flag directly: four 6-pointed stars on a white field between two blue stripes. Changed from marigold to Chicago flag red (#cc0024). Four stars, not three, not five.
Thrift as a seventh filter Eight curated thrift and vintage spots — Reckless Records, Village Discount, Knee Deep Vintage, Broadway Antique Market, Ragstock, Dusty Groove, The Brown Elephant, Lost Eras — added to the existing 25 spots. Thrift gets its own color token (#7c5a2a, warm leather brown) distinct from the four Pilsen accents.
No header imagery V24 added price tag SVGs to the header — V25 removes them. The Pilsen palette's rainbow border-image carries the decorative weight without additional illustration.
